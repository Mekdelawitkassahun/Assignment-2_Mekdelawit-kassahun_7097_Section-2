About mekdi Restaurant

Welcome to Mekdi Restaurant, located on Queen Elizabeth Street in the heart of Addis Ababa. We are dedicated to providing an exceptional dining experience by offering a menu inspired by the finest local ingredients and a passion for traditional flavors with modern flair. Each dish is crafted with care, ensuring that every bite is an unforgettable experience.

At Mekdi Restaurant, we believe that food is more than just a meal—it's a way to bring people together. Our talented team, led by Chef Nahom, is committed to delivering outstanding service in a warm, inviting atmosphere. Whether you're enjoying a casual lunch, celebrating a special occasion, or sharing a quiet dinner with friends, we strive to make every visit memorable. Our menu features a variety of dishes that cater to all tastes, paired with a carefully curated selection of wines and beverages. Every detail is designed with you in mind, and we take pride in making sure every guest feels at home. Thank you for choosing Mekdi Restaurant. We look forward to welcoming you soon and sharing our passion for delicious food and exceptional hospitality.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional dining experiences with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients, combining tradition and innovation in a welcoming, family-friendly atmosphere.
